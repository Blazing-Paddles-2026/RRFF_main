Who We Are

About the Round Rock Fire Foundation

Founded by fire families, for fire families. The Round Rock Fire Foundation supports the firefighters of the Round Rock Fire Department, their families, and the broader community with critical resources, programs, and enduring partnerships.

Our Story

Built around a kitchen table.

The Foundation was created by fire families who saw the need to extend the department's long tradition of caring for its own. What began as conversations about how to strengthen and support fire families year-round grew into a foundation built on community, compassion, and commitment.

Today, RRFF carries that vision forward — ensuring firefighters and their families are cared for in lasting, meaningful ways, from the start of a career through retirement.

Our Mission

The Round Rock Fire Foundation provides resources, care, and community-driven programs that support fire families from the start of their careers to retirement, ensuring they thrive in every aspect of their lives.

What We Do

Three pillars of support.

Family Support

Crisis relief, wellness programs, and peer connection for fire families when they need it most.

Health & Resilience

Mental health resources, family-focused wellness, and training that supports the whole firefighter.

Legacy & Support

Uniforms, gear, and programs that honor the tradition of service — past, present, and future.

What Makes Us Different

Founded by fire families, for fire families.

Our programs focus on family integration, resilience, and long-term care. We work in support of the Round Rock Fire Department, helping highlight its national example of how family-centered policies strengthen both firefighters and the people who stand beside them.
