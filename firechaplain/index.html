Chap's Corner.

A chaplain-led blog and support resource for Round Rock fire families — encouragement, reflection, and care-centered guidance from the Chaplain who walks with our firefighters and their loved ones.

John Patterson

Fire Chaplain

"With faith-centered care and intentional support, firefighters can finish their careers stronger, healthier, and more connected than when they began."

Led by Chaplain Patterson's pastoral voice, Chap's Corner offers encouragement, reflection, and care for fire families. The Round Rock Fire Foundation is proud to connect families to this trusted source of support.

About this resource

Chap's Corner is a chaplain-run blog and support resource that the Round Rock Fire Foundation links to but does not manage. The views and reflections shared are Chaplain Patterson's own, offered freely to the fire community he serves.

The Foundation is proud to walk alongside Chap and the families he supports through The 1884 Fund and the work of our volunteers.
