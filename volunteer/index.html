Join Our Crew

Volunteer with RRFF.

Our signature events run on volunteer power. Whether you can give a few hours at Foundation Night, work check-in tables at Blazing Paddles, or help host the Spouse Conference — we have a spot for you.

Where we need help

Volunteer roles change by event, but here's what tends to come up most:

  • Event check-in & registration tables
  • Setup, breakdown, and day-of logistics
  • Fundraising & outreach support
  • Community engagement and sponsor relations
  • Family support and Spouse Conference hospitality

How it works

Sign up below with a quick note about what you'd like to help with. We'll follow up by email with the next volunteer call when it matches.

No minimum commitment, no recurring shifts — show up for what fits your schedule.

We'll respond by email. No spam — we only reach out when there's a real volunteer need.
