Meet our Board.

Our board brings together fire department leadership, financial and community advocates, and the chaplain who walks alongside our firefighters every day. Together, they steward the mission of the Round Rock Fire Foundation.

A volunteer board of seven, balancing operational expertise with community leadership — chosen for their commitment to fire families and their roots in Round Rock.
